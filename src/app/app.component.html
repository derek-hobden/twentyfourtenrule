The Twenty-Four-Ten Rule

What is it?

Be smart when deciding how much to spend on a car. Follow this rule:

  • Twenty - Put down 20% deposit, at least.
  • Four - Finance the car up to 4 years, but no more.
  • Ten - The remaining costs should not exceed 10% of your salary.

If you have to break ANY of these rules, you cannot afford the car.
